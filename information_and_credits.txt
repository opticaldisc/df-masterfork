---------------------------------------- MASTERFORK GENERATIVE AI POLICY ----------------------------------------

  I shall NEVER allow MASTERFORK to be sullied by generative AI content of the following nature:
  
 - sprites (both UI and/or creature tiles)
 - sound of any kind
 - anything graphical what-so-ever
 - RAWS that are written in its ENTIRETY, or in large by generative AI
 
   conditionally allowed:
 * RAWS that are written in its ENTIRETY, or certain allowed OBJECTS which follows:
 1. {TEXT_SET}
 2. {LANGUAGE}
 generative content in these raws I belive are fine as they do not go against the spirit of the game-
 and are UNDER THE CONDITION that each entry is manually reviewed for quality and consistency.
