squamous's notes on armor spawning:
	for NPCS, 
	1. Armorlevel 1 will always be used by ranged fighters
	2. Armorlevel 2 by default will always be used by melee fighters
	3. Armorlevel 3 will never be used outside of rule 4
	4. HOWEVER NPC civs will assign a helmet and body armor [to the civilization uniform], either of which is completely random and ignores any ARMORLEVEL logic, which is how ARMORLEVEL:3 items can be used
